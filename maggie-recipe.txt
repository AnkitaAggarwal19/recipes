Ingredients:
    - maggie 
    - maggie masala
    - 1 1/2 cups of water
    - salt
    - Cut vegetables
        - 1 onion
        - 1 tomato
        - 1 simla mirch

Directions:
  1. Take 1 1/2 cups of water in a pan.
  2. Add maggie masala to it.
  3. Wait for it to boil.
  4. Add salt to taste.
  5. Add maggie to it at once.
  6. Add vegetables to it.
